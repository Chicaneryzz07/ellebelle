Chapter I

About Her

◆

❧

She is the kind of person that authors dream of writing — a soul composed of equal parts quiet wonder and fierce, burning feeling. There is a softness to her, like the turning of a well-worn page, and yet within her lives entire worlds, each more beautiful and mysterious than the last.

Her smile is the kind that fills a room without knowing it, and her eyes — they read people the way she reads books: with patience, with care, with the desire to understand every hidden chapter. She collects moments like pressed flowers between pages, keeping them safe long after the world has moved on.

She is stubborn in the most endearing way, gentle in ways she does not realize, and brilliant in ways she will never fully believe. She makes ordinary evenings feel like scenes from a beloved novel, and ordinary mornings feel like the beginning of something extraordinary.

To know her is to find yourself quoted in the margins of someone else's favourite story.

"She is a complete world unto herself — and I am grateful to be a small part of it."
